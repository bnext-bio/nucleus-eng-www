Nucleus Hub

A digital environment for building synthetic cells

Nucleus Hub is a Jupyter environment that enables developers to share and analyze data in the cloud, and disseminate data stories through DevNotes.

When you launch a dataset, you will initially be shown a low-resolution version of all wells imaged in the experiment. Clicking on an individual well will open the full-resolution dataset for that well. Some wells will require you to adjust the exposure of the channels (using the sliders on the left) in order to see detail in the image.

Collaborative Workspace

Work individually, in small groups, or publicly, asynchronously or synchronously with shared workspaces. DevNotes can be published from the working environment.

Pre-loaded Tools

Set up with the Nucleus Cell Development Kit, which includes plate reader and microscopy tools, for digitally specifying and analyzing cytosols and cells.
